Enough Patients

  • This is something often overlooked. For example, you’re really interested in postoperative length of stay in elderly (age >80) males having inguinal hernia repairs? Assume you want to know the median LOS within ± 5% (with 95% confidence).
  • As noted above, this would require gathering data on over 300 patients. Do we really have 300 patients available to us? And what happens if you need to exclude some patients (not eligible)?
  • To answer this question, you will always need to do some homework.
