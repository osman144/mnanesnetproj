Narrative Protocol

In essence, this is the “methods section” for your eventual paper. It should be “chronologic” (first we do this, then we do that....). I should be detailed so that someone else can easily “visualize” what your doing. But beware, IRB’s sometimes misinterpret things and may confuse “experiment” for “routine care”. If everyone in your study population already gets and arterial catheter placed routinely, just say “blood pressure will be recorded from routinely placed arterial catheters” (so IRB doesn’t get into “do you need experimental consent to place an arterial catheter?”). Spell out in clear terms WHEN drugs and interventions will take place and when measurements will be taken in the course of the study.
