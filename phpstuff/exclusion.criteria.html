Exclusion Criteria

Also usually straightforward. But I’ve seen more nonsense in exclusion criteria than I care to count. Be reasonable. It isn’t necessary to say “we’re going to exclude patients on LVADs” if your doing a simple regional anesthesia study in the ASC! Stick with the exclusion criteria that you’re likely to encounter in real life. Yes, you’ll run into someone with characteristics that ARE NOT on your exclusion list. As long as these are “uncommon or unexpected”, you can still exclude them without their being on the list.
