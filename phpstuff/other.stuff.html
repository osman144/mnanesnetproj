Exclusion Criteria

  • This should be self-explanatory. But be honest and as accurate as possible. If you need the help of an RA think carefully about how much time will be needed per patient for the tasks that are needed. Don’t forget the “obligatory admin time”.
  • I may take 30min to consent a patient – but how many patient records and how much time are need to be reviewed to IDENTIFY an eligible patient?
  • It may take 1hr to gather the data on a patient – but if the RA is going to enter this into your database, how much time will that take? Is the RA time “feasible”?
