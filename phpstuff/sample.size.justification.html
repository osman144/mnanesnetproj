Sample Size Justification

  • A sample size estimate must be based on your best guesstimate (based on real information) of the average/median value of your control variable, the variance around that central tendency, the magnitude of the effect you hope to see (and prove), the POWER you wish to have to detect that effect-size (usually 0.8 or 0.9) and the p-value that you are going to accept (usually 0.05).
